📖 HOW TO PLAY
🎯 The Goal
Score the most points by answering sports trivia. Pick a sport, dial in your settings, then tap tiles on the board to reveal questions.
🎮 Game Modes
In Person — A host reads each clue aloud and marks answers right or wrong. Best for a group around one screen or TV.

Solo — Play on your own. Every question is multiple choice and graded automatically.

Multiplayer — Teams share one device and pass it around. Each team answers multiple-choice questions on their turn.
🟦 The Board
Categories run across the top; point values (100–400) down the side. The higher the points, the tougher the question. Tap any tile to play it.
💎 Diamond Double
The light-blue tiles are Diamond Doubles. Answer correctly and you earn double the points — but a wrong answer costs you double too. Risk and reward!
🏆 Final Round
Once the board is cleared, every team wagers points on one last question. Bet wisely — the lead can change in an instant.
⏱️ Timer & Difficulty
Each question runs on a countdown clock. Choose from four difficulty levels — Beginner to Expert — to match your crew.
